Hammersmith Ukulele Group

Amateur ukulele night every 2nd and 4th Monday at the Stonemasons Arms in Hammersmith

Who is HUG?

We're just people who show up and make music!

Anyone is welcome to drop by and join in. If you don't have an ukulele, we're happy to have other accompaniment. We can always use more voices to join in and sing!

We have an e-mail mailing list to help you stay up-to-date with the next events and more.

Join the Mailing List

What songs do HUG play?

Our songbook is a work in progress, and we're always open to suggestions.

You can look over our Song sheets page to read or print out individual songs, or download the whole book via the button below.

Where do HUG meet?

We play at The Stonemasons Arms in Hammersmith, London.

You can usually find us in the "function room" on the top floor: just grab a drink and head up the stairs in back.

When do HUG meet?

We are currently meeting on the second and fourth Monday of each month.

Check our calendar for the next HUG Jam date.

The function room opens at 7:30pm, and we tend to play until closing. Drop in any time, but we tend to play the easier songs toward the start of the evening.
