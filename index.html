Hammersmith Ukulele Group!

Just a bunch of people meeting forthnightishly for a strum.

Who

We are amateurs and welcome beginners. Bring an ukulele if you can, if not we may have one spare for you.

Where

You can find us in West London: we usually meet at The Stonemason's Arms.

When

We are currently meeting on the second and fourth Monday of each month at 7:30pm.
