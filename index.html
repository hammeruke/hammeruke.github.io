Hammersmith Ukulele Group

Amateur ukulele night every 2nd and 4th Monday at the Stonemason's Arms in Hammersmith

All are welcome!

Who are we

We're just people who show up and make music!

Anyone is welcome to drop by and join in. If you don't have an ukulele, we're happy to have other accompaniment. We can always use more voices to join in and sing!

Where do we meet?

We play at The Stonemason's Arms, in Hammersmith, London. You can usually find us in the "function room" on the top floor: just grab a drink and head up the stairs in back.

When do we meet?

We are currently meeting on the second and fourth Monday of each month. Check our calendar for the next HUG Jam date.

The function room opens at 7:30pm, and we tend to play until closing. Drop in any time, but we tend to play the easier songs toward the start of the evening.
